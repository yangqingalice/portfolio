Savor
Book

What does art taste like?

Savor is a cookbook that contains five recipes for dishes as representation and recreation of a selection of artworks from the contemporary gallery of RISD Museum.

The designer created these dishes based on her sensory experience of the museum and each piece of art. With a collection of ingredients and casual instructions, readers are able to recreate these dishes with their own interpretation of the instructions. With an artistic hand, everyone creates their own unique edible art.

Box of A Month
Chocolate Packaging

Chocolates have been used as gift for many special occasions, holidays and birthdays, etc. This is a packaging design for chocolates to celebrate or memorize a special month of a year. Here May is used as a demonstration of the packaging system.

The small book hidden inside the box is a piece of poem celebrating the month, but it can also be a note to the receiver.

The wood surface of the box is carefully stained and sanded, giving a smooth and soft touch.

Sundance Film Festival
Film Editing

This is a promotion video for 2017 Sundance Film Festival, an independent film festival in USA. Sundance Institute, found by Robert Redford in 1981, celebrates and curates independent films and stories. This film is a composition of carefully selected clips of films curated by Sundance Institute in 2016.

Map of Dreams
Book

This is a re-interpretation of Freud Sigmund's famous book, The Interpretation of Dreams.

It creates a whimsical reading experience with the selection of "dream" words and punctuations. Printed on translucent paper, the material gives a natural gradient and depth.

/SHaDou/
Book

This piece is an exploration of light and shadow, form and content. The content, which is the dictionary definition of "shadow", is set through four pieces of 3d typography and bound into a book.

Instead of laser cutting, the designer chose to cut out precisely with hand to avoid the burnt mark on the edge, eliminating color distractions and focus the viewers on the form and shadow created by the structure.
